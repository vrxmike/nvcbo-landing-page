Who We Are

Northern Vision Community-Based Organization (NVCBO) is a grassroots organization based in Northern Kenya, working with indigenous and pastoralist communities in Arid and Semi-Arid Lands (ASALs). We combine indigenous knowledge, community leadership and practical solutions to advance climate justice, gender equality, education and sustainable development.
