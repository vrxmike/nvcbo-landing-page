Our Methodology

Healing Circles

Healing circles are a core methodology across all Northern Vision programs. They are used for community dialogue, participatory planning, conflict resolution, youth mentorship and internal organizational reflection.

Northern Vision CBO

Northern Vision CBO is a grassroots organization advancing climate adaptation, education, gender justice and peacebuilding in Northern Kenya through community-led solutions, healing circles and storytelling.
