NorthernVision: Building a future beyond boundaries

Get Involved Today

NorthernVision Community Based Organization was founded in 2020 with major focus on SDGs 15, 5 and 10.

We place special emphasis on empowering smallholder farmers, particularly women and youth, by providing comprehensive support. Our strategy integrates sustainable agricultural practices, climate resilience measures, technical and digital skill training, and robust financial empowerment initiatives.
