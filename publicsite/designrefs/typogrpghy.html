Theory of Change

Change begins at the
grassroots

At NorthernVision CBO, we believe communities are transformed when grassroots women have the power to lead. Through collective action, we build resilient, locally driven solutions to combat climate challenges and empower the marginalized.

01

Grassroots Women are the Foundation

Lasting transformation begins with women. Through their lived experiences, indigenous knowledge, and hands-on leadership, they drive vital changes directly within their local communities.

02

Shifting Power to Communities

We sustain long-term impact by shifting resource allocation, decision-making, and strategic authority directly into indigenous networks situated across Northern Kenya.
