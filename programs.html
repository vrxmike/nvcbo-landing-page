Our Programs

Addressing climate change, gender inequality, education gaps, and peace through community-led action.

1. Climate Adaptation & Food Security

To strengthen community resilience through sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, and locally led adaptation.

Key Components
  • Community Farm: Solar pumps, fish farming, and Azolla breeding.
  • Regreening: Tree planting in schools and homesteads.
  • Renewable Energy: Solar/wind solutions to reduce fossil fuel use.
Healing Circles Role: Used to co-design adaptation solutions and strengthen community ownership.

2. Education & Culture

Expanding access to education, mentorship, and cultural knowledge while strengthening identity.

Key Components
  • Youth Mentorship: Building skills and confidence for marginalized youth.
  • Cultural Heritage: Documenting indigenous knowledge through storytelling.
Healing Circles Role: Facilitating intergenerational knowledge sharing and youth leadership reflection.

3. Sexual & Reproductive Health (SRHR)

Promoting dignity, health, and informed decision-making among adolescents and women.

Key Components
  • Menstrual Health: Education and sanitary product distribution.
  • Community Dialogue: Addressing FGC and harmful practices respectfully.
  • Adolescent Empowerment: Linking health to education and livelihoods.
Healing Circles Role: Providing safe spaces for sensitive conversations on rights and dignity.

4. Peace & Security

Strengthening social cohesion and preventing conflict through dialogue and digital literacy.

Key Components
  • Digital Literacy: Combating misinformation and online incitement.
  • Storytelling for Peace: Amplifying voices that promote coexistence.
Healing Circles Role: Mediating dialogue and building trust across diverse groups.
