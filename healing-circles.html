Healing Circles

A culturally grounded methodology for engagement, dialogue, and wellbeing.

What Are Healing Circles?

Healing circles are a culturally grounded methodology used by Northern Vision CBO for community engagement, dialogue, learning, and organizational wellbeing. They create safe spaces for collective reflection, conflict resolution, and community-led problem-solving.

How We Use Healing Circles

  • Community engagement and participatory planning
  • Dialogue on sensitive social and cultural issues
  • Conflict resolution and peacebuilding
  • Youth mentorship and leadership reflection
  • Internal staff reflection, learning, and wellbeing
  • Developing research and training modules
